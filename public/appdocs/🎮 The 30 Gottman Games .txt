🎮 The 30 Gottman Games (with Marcie as Host)
1. 🎯 “Bid Radar”
Mechanics: Each partner logs real-world bids they made or received today (e.g., “sighed while cooking,” “texted a meme”). AI cross-matches: Did Partner A notice Partner B’s “casual glance at the wine cabinet”?
Gameplay:
* 60-second “Field Report” submission window.
* Marcie reveals mismatches with sass: “Oh, you thought ‘staring mournfully at the trash can’ was a cry for help? Cute. They just wanted you to take it out.”
* Points for accuracy, not volume.
Marcie’s Role: Narrator + Judge + Truth Bomb Dropper. Voice lines vary by score (e.g., low score → “Honey, we need to recalibrate your emotional Wi-Fi.”)
________________


2. 💬 “Gentle Start-Up Gauntlet”
Mechanics: Marcie presents a harsh starter (“You never listen!”). Players race to rewrite it using I feel… about… I need… in <20 sec. AI grades tone, structure, and vulnerability level.
Gameplay: 5 rounds. Bonus points for poetic phrasing.
Marcie’s Role: Host + Grammar Nazi + Empathy Coach. “Ooh, ‘I feel like a solo podcast host’ — creative, but stick to the script, Picasso.”
________________


3. 🧠 “Love Map Speedrun”
Mechanics: Kahoot-style live quiz: “What’s their go-to comfort snack this month?” “Name two people they vented to last week.” Answers pulled from their shared journal (opt-in sync).
Gameplay: Timed, 10 Qs. Speed + accuracy = points.
Marcie’s Role: Quizmaster + Memory Keeper. “They changed their fave ice cream three weeks ago. If you missed that, you’re emotionally GPS-less.”
________________


4. 🛡️ “Antidote Arena”
Mechanics: Marcie plays a Four Horsemen audio clip (AI-voiced, exaggerated). Players pick the correct antidote (Gentle Start-Up? Self-Soothing?).
Gameplay: Real-time buzz-in. Streaks unlock “Repair Power-Ups” (e.g., “Time-Out Token”).
Marcie’s Role: Drama Director. “Contempt level: chef’s kiss. Now neutralize it before I lose faith in humanity.”
________________


5. 🪞 “Mirror Mode”
Mechanics: Partners record 15-second video responses to prompts (“Describe me in 3 words — no repeats from last month!”). AI analyzes tone, smiles, word choice.
Gameplay: Guess which word your partner used for you. Points for match + emotional alignment (e.g., “warm” + smile = bonus).
Marcie’s Role: Stylist + Truth-Teller. “You said ‘resilient’ — and your voice cracked. I’m filing that under ‘adorable.’”
________________


6. 🕵️ “Dream Decoder”
Mechanics: Each secretly picks a surface conflict (e.g., “They leave dishes in sink”). AI guides partner to guess the underlying dream (e.g., “I crave order to feel safe”).
Gameplay: 3 clues max. Points for speed + depth.
Marcie’s Role: Psychoanalyst + Whisperer. “The dishes aren’t about dishes, darling. They’re about control. Let’s not pretend.”
________________


7. 🎭 “Tone Shift Challenge”
Mechanics: Read a neutral sentence (“We need to talk.”) in 4 tones: Sarcastic, Anxious, Warm, Playful. AI scores vocal warmth (via mic analysis). Partner guesses tone.
Gameplay: Blind listen → guess. Bonus if AI + partner agree.
Marcie’s Role: Vocal Coach + Sass Queen. “That ‘warm’ sounded like a robot ordering coffee. Try again, Siri.”
________________


8. 🧩 “Ritual Builder”
Mechanics: Drag-and-drop ritual ingredients (☕ + 📖 + 🐾 = “Morning Coffee + News + Pet Cuddles”). AI suggests improvements.
Gameplay: Build 3 rituals. Partner rates “Likelihood to Actually Happen” (1–5). High realism = points.
Marcie’s Role: Lifestyle Architect. “‘Midnight stargazing’? Cute. Add ‘blanket’ and ‘no mosquitoes’ or I’m revoking your romance license.”
________________


9. 🎲 “Conflict Dice”
Mechanics: Roll virtual dice → get conflict scenario + constraint (“Argue about thermostat — but no ‘you’ statements!”). Record 30-sec resolution. AI scores for antidotes used.
Gameplay: Async. Top 3 clips featured in “Hall of Fame.”
Marcie’s Role: Referee + Hype Woman. “Defensiveness detected! Penalty lap: Say ‘I contributed by…’ three times.”
________________


10. 💌 “Appreciation Auction”
Mechanics: Each writes 3 appreciations. AI anonymizes. Partners bid “Emotional Coins” to claim the real ones. Fake ones (AI-generated decoys) cost coins if chosen.
Gameplay: Strategy + bluffing. Highest net coins wins.
Marcie’s Role: Auctioneer + Forger. “Lot 3: ‘You fold laundry like a Zen master.’ Is it real? Or did I write that? Place your bets…”
________________


11. 🧘 “Flooding Forecast”
Mechanics: Heart rate (via phone cam) + self-report during mock conflict. AI predicts “flooding risk.” Partner guesses your calm-down method.
Gameplay: Calm-down mini-game (e.g., box breathing). Points for accuracy + speed.
Marcie’s Role: Biometric Analyst. “Your heart’s doing salsa. Suggest: 20 minutes, a walk, and no texting me your grievances.”

12. 🗺️ “Love Map Gap Quest”
Mechanics: AI finds “gaps” in your shared Love Map (e.g., “They added ‘pottery’ to hobbies — you haven’t asked about it”). Partner crafts one curious Q to close it.
Gameplay: Best Q (AI + partner rated) wins.
Marcie’s Role: Cartographer. “Darling, your Love Map has a ‘Here Be Dragons’ zone. Time to explore.”

13. 🎨 “Shared Meaning Mural”
Mechanics: Collaborative digital mural: Add symbols, colors, words representing “us.” AI detects alignment (e.g., both add “mountains” = shared adventure value).
Gameplay: 5-min live co-creation. Sync score = points.
Marcie’s Role: Art Critic. “You both chose ‘storm clouds with rainbows.’ Trauma-bonding? Or just poetic? I’m invested.”

14. 📞 “Text Tone Translator”
Mechanics: Upload real (anonymized) texts. AI flags “risk tones” (e.g., period = passive-aggressive?). Rewrite safer versions.
Gameplay: Partner guesses which rewrite is “most likely to get a ❤️.”
Marcie’s Role: Linguist. “‘K.’ sent at 11:47 PM? That’s not a text — it’s a war crime.”

15. 🏆 “Repair Relay”
Mechanics: Marcie describes a conflict escalation. Players race to type the next best repair attempt. AI ranks by empathy + feasibility.
Gameplay: 4 rounds. Team score = fastest and highest-ranked repairs.
Marcie’s Role: Crisis Manager. “They’re stonewalling. Your move: Humor? Touch? Or just hand them wine and walk away? GO.”
