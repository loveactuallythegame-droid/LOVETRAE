Linking your partner is easy! Go to Settings > Profile > Link Partner. You will receive a unique cosmic code to share. Once they enter the code on their device, your hearts will be synced in real-time.
SOS Mode is a specialized de-escalation tool designed for high-tension moments. When activated by either partner, it locks competitive mini-games and offers communication prompts, breathing exercises, and collaborative tasks to help you reconnect calmly.
