Privacy Policy

Your intimacy and data security are our cosmic priority. We treat your connection with the same reverence as the stars.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Love Actually... The Game. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe and secure environment for your therapeutic journey. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide while using our game.

Our goal is to help couples strengthen their bond through cosmic-themed exercises, and we understand that the data generated during this process is deeply personal and sensitive.

2. Data Collection

We collect information that allows us to provide a personalized experience for you and your partner:

  • Account Information: Names, email addresses, and account credentials used for authentication.
  • Game Progress: Your choices, answers to intimacy prompts, and completion status of therapy modules.
  • Device Metadata: Information about the device used to access the game for technical optimization and crash reporting.
  • Communication: Feedback and messages sent to our support team.

3. Data Usage

Your data isn't just numbers to us; it's the map to your relationship's galaxy. We use this information to:

Personalization

Tailoring the therapy prompts based on your relationship history and preferences.

Progress Tracking

Visualizing your growth as a couple through cosmic charts and milestone rewards.

App Optimization

Improving game performance and squashing bugs to ensure a seamless experience.

Legal Compliance

Ensuring we meet international standards for data protection and privacy.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We believe your business is yours alone. We never sell your private therapy data or personal identifiers to third-party advertisers.

Disclosure only occurs in very limited circumstances: with your explicit consent, with trusted service providers who help us run the game (under strict confidentiality), or if required by law to protect our users or comply with a legal process.

5. Security

Our defense systems are built on "Cosmic-Level Encryption." We use AES-256 bit encryption to ensure that your data is scrambled and unreadable to anyone without proper authorization.

Secure by Design

Our servers are housed in Tier-4 data centers with 24/7 monitoring. We regularly conduct penetration tests to find and fix potential vulnerabilities before they can be exploited.
