Billing Cycles & Auto-Renewal

Subscriptions are billed at the beginning of each cycle. Monthly plans renew every 30 days. Yearly plans are billed as a single upfront payment and renew annually. All subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the period ends.

Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time through your account dashboard or app store settings. Upon cancellation, you will retain access to Pro features until the end of your current billing period. No partial refunds are provided.
